- *Reduced* ring

    A ring $R$ is *reduced* $\iff R$ has no nonzero nilpotent elements $\iff x^2  = 0 \implies x=0$.

- Nakayama's Lemma

    If $M \in \rmod$ is finitely generated and $I\normal R$ with $I \subseteq \mcj(R)$, then $$IM = M \implies M = 0.$$
